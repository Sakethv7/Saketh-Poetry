Living as a wandering poet, believing, as I walk away from yesterday.

Which version would you be, my destiny?

She still, carelessly, without contention —
cuts through my frail heart and its reason.

O my dear melancholy,
how much have I missed you?

I was reborn when I saw her
at the turn of a hill.

After many seasons,
my umbrella found a companion.

It's not as if I can't help but admit
that this ballad is real.

Ye ghazal saboot hai ki ye pyaar hi to hai.
