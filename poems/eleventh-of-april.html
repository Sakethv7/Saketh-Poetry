11th of April

A date that stays in the blood.

I

I saunter among the autumn

leaves that have turned brown

while I write a melancholic poem

about my life, as I foolishly drown.

◆
II

I drown in these nonsensical

emotions that do overwhelm

me, and my pen which remain fatal

on this rainy day, that I condemn.

◆
III

I take refuge under a lonely shade

and I ponder about the signal

that my heart has for the dead

feelings that forcefully linger.

◆
IV

I place my hands onto the wood

that has become fragile with time

I realised that I never understood

how a caterpillar could climb.

◆
V

I see raindrops falling on a flower

that seeps through the shade

leaving no space for any answer

about life, that I wanted in charade.

◆
VI

While I was searching for clarity

I stumbled on this rainy and peaceful

place that makes me forget reality

but I only write a rhyme too painful.

◆
VII

11th of April, I had gotten a message

from her that was hurting in tandem

with the thoughts that lay vestige

in my mind and in my sorrowful poem.

◆
VIII

Now it's a rainy day of August

a month that only makes promises

of cold and chilly winds with trust

but now it rains on flowers of hibiscus.

 

I hear the ticking of my watch

that is damp from the frisky rain

and I crave for a rocks on scotch

so that I could feel like a swain.

 

I finish this poem that was due

and I walk past a moist flower

with an umbrella that is blue

which she gave me on a September.

 

I put the poem inside the bag

and I open the rusty parasol

to protect the bag from the rain

as it has a poem and a photograph.

 

It's a poem about her

and a photograph of us.

A date that stays in the blood.
