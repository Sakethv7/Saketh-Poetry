Aangan Ke Phool

When the courtyard starts to bloom again.

I

Aangan ke taraf aankh bhatakti reheti hai

Lagta hai ek yaad si aakar bethi hai.

◆
II

Patjhad ke girte patte udaas lagte hai

Jaane girne se pehele hi baat keh dete hai.

◆
III

Tanhai ke ghadi mein kisi ka saath nahi

Jinn phoolon ko itna sajaya wo bhi murjhaye hai.

◆
IV

Paani chaaro taraf daalne ke baad bhi

Bageeche mein ek hi phool dikhai deta hai.

◆
V

Naye phoolon ke beej lag jaye bhi to

Vo puraane phoolon ki yaad hi karaate hai.

◆
VI

Roz aziyat mein doobe raston pe bhatakte hai

Ab patjhad se kitni hi ummeed kar sakte hai.

◆
VII

Himmat nahi haari hai naye dinon ki khalvat mein

Bahaar ke phool kabhi to baraste hai.

◆
VIII

Phir bhatakti hai meri aankhe aangan mein

Lagta hai koi naya aane ko hai.

एक नज़्म — आँगन के फूल।
