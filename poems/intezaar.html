Intezaar

The only thing that stays.

Dil mein ab soz-e-intezaar to raha nahi

Tumne ye hi baat meri maan li thi kya.

Bheegi raatein meri aankhon ki nami chhipa rahi hai

Ab aankhon mein tujhe mehfooz rakhna bhi gunnah hai kya.

Mere sab ishq-e-alfaaz beasar hi rahe

Main koi alag desh jaa chuka hoon kya.

Itne haseen chehre hai to mere ird gird

Par tum jaisa koi milna mumkin bhi hai kya.

Ye patjhad bhi mujhe chain kyon nahi de raha

Ek hi shaks tha jahaan mein kya.

Ye pal hai jo guzara nahi jaa raha humse

Ab humne wo zindagi nahi guzari kya.

Kalam to maujud rahega jab koi naya bhi aaye

Par tum jaisi koi alqa iss aalam mein hai kya.

Naye kaliyon se fir mulaqat karne nikle ke kahi tera roop dikh jaye

Par aise haalat bhi humse jaan pehechan rakhte hai kya.

एक ग़ज़ल — इंतेज़ार।
