Nazm

Patjhad Ka Parichay

How autumn introduces itself.

I

Aaj ye thandi hawa apna

Parichaya karta hai mujhse

Aur mere kalam se jo dikhata hai

Khwab mere dil ko iss suhani raat.

◆
II

Chaudavi ka chandh iss sanjh

Rachta hai har shayar ke liye

Aisi saajish jo karde shahad

Ki mukhi ko bhi sharminda.

◆
III

Beheka bahar hi jaanta hai jo

Mujhe mila gaya tha ik shayar

Ke khwab se jisne mere

Mohabbat ki dastaan palat di.

◆
IV

Taras gaye hai meri aankhein

Ab uss haseena ko dekhne mein

Jo mere khwab mein aakar meri

Nazm mein shaamil ho gayi.

◆
V

Ab manzil reh gaye hai anjaan

Jo kuch mausam pehle the

Jankaar, aur awaara reh gaya

Hai ye dil, jo tha pehele samajhdar.

◆
VI

Kagazo mein uski taareef

Barkaraar hai aur iss chandni

Raat ko wo leti hai tashrif

Mere dil ki suhaani kavita mein.

◆
VII

Mile the hum ik chhote mod pe

Jo tha khila bahaar ke phoolo

Se, par ab wo sadak mein sirf

Reh gaye, murjhaye hue phool.

◆
VIII

Dil jalati thi wo mera, apni

Yaari sabke liye dikhake

Mein haath malte reh gaya

Aur shaamein dhalte gaye.

◆
IX

Ik baar phirse ban gaye

Hum dono ishq mein ajnabi

Jab pata chala mujhe ki uski

Kashmo kashm mein tha anjaana.

◆
X

Lakh haseen dekhe the

Par ussa nahi dekha tha

Ab bas hai wo shayar ka khwab

Aur ek kavi ki bahar-e-mehboob.

◆
XI

Iss raat pedo ki shaakho pe aisi

Khamoshi chhayi jo use yaad

Karne mein karta hai majboor

Aur uski yaadein na hoti riha.

◆
XII

Kuch palo ka hai ye guztaakh sama

Kabhi ho payegi wo mujhse juda?

Bas lotkar na aye kaafile bahar ke

Chalo firse banaya ajnabi zindagi ne.

एक नज़्म — पतझड़ का परिचय।
