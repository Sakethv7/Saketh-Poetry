Shaam-e-Gham

Sorrow of the Evening

Mujhe har waqt yaad aata hai

Ke maine tumhe bhula diya.

Baarish ke boond sawaal karte hai

Jawaab jinke lafzon ne daba diya.

Mayusi theher gayi hai dil mein

Aisi bekhudi ka sabab hawa mein uda diya.

Meri har koshish ishq ki nakaam hi rahi

Itna tajurba lekar bhi khud ko hara diya.

Meri zillat safar karegi aur kuch door

Ek nadi kinare iss khat ka naav bana diya.

Tum jao aur dariya dariya pyaas bhujao

Ab kisi aur ke dil ko samjhota karao.

Hume bhi shauq na tha dastaan sunane ka

Taqalluf mein usne to aise hi puch diya.

Pyaar se 'Saketh' bulaake taqalluf mein daal diya

Ye dil jo toot gaya use naam se bulaake fir jaga diya.

शाम का दर्द, रात भर का प्रश्न।
