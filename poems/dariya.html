Ghazal

Dariya

The River. A strange affliction called love.

Ye dillagi bhi kaisi anokhi bimari hai

Itne din beet gaye tab bhi yaad suhani hai.

Tez hawa ne mujhse pucha

Iss mohabbat se sawaal puche sabne hai.

Humse na pucho ye beete hijr ke kisse

Har kisse mein unki beparwahi ka zikr hai.

Ye cigarette ka dhua le aata hai saath samundar paar

Hume kya pata tha ye yaadein bhi beheti hai.

Ab kis khushakismat ko tum chaahton se dekhte ho

Barbaadi dekh kar bhi hum haat malte hai.

Hum ghazal mein tumhara naam nahi zikr hone dete

Inn yaad bhare khaton ko rusvan nahi hone dete hai.

Ye jaam roz rooh ko pyasi kar deti hai

Pyaas hi sahi phir ye udaasi kahan se dhundh laati hai.

प्रेम की नदी में बहते हुए।
