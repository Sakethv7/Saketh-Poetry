Dusky Window

Time passed — transparent as the gushing wind, hollow in its wake.

I

The time that passed is like a dusky window

It seemed transparent like the gushing wind

But what has passed remains hollow

From the emotions that seldom rescind.

◆
II

You can see through the memory

But it's the emotion that brings storms

And as it all becomes a melancholy

The reminiscence slowly deforms.

◆
III

Conversing about time that has passed

Is an activity for people who are strife

Who find time that has amassed

In this maze seldom called life.

◆
IV

As the cigarette leaves my lips

I ponder if memories are canned by fate

In the store of my subconscious which slips

When it returns memories with an expiry date.

◆
V

I exhale the smoke while reminiscing a time

When she strolled beside me in October

Her hair sailed in the air by the wind

Blowing away like my time with her.

◆
VI

I stroll on the forgotten street

With worn out houses but new doors

Making me wonder if I could meet

Her again on these nostalgic crossroads.

◆
VII

Now it's all a memory to cherish

As most of them should be

And when this soul does perish

I'll choose to vanish with this memory.

A memory with an expiry date — and a soul that chooses to vanish with it.
