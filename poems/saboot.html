Saboot

Proof. A classical ghazal form.

Raat bhar tumhari yaad se ghire hi to hai

Us yaad mein shamm-e-gham jalaya hi to hai.

Chala tha zikr bewafai ka inn baharo mein

So tumhara khayal aya magar bhulana hi to hai.

Tumse baat hue ek zamaana hua magar

Us hasi ko ojhal hona abhi baaki hi to hai.

Hum par be-dilli ka ilzaam hi to hai

Iqrar hi sahi muhabbat azaab hi to hai.

Koi jurm to nahi tumse anjaan-e-ishq karna

Tumhara hum pe waqt guzarna aziyat hi to hai.

Kitna be-bas karega ab ye teri-meri chuppi

Jaan-e-jaan ye ghazal mein tera naam hi to hai.

Tera khil jana mujhse baat karke sachai hi to hai

Tera yun baat talna har baat pe haan hi to hai.

Ab inn hothon pe naam tera to nahi

Fir bhi bahare tera pata dhundte hi to hai.

Kitne hi dil-farebi reh jao tum 'Saketh'

Ye ghazal saboot hai ki ye pyaar hi to hai.

एक गज़ल जो प्रेम का प्रमाण है।
