Almost Blue

A saxophone's confession.

I

I am almost blue

and maybe just because of you

why does it feel to be true

that I won't ever find someone like you.

◆
II

A mind as empty as mine

feels a remorse that none can subside

I think if I move towards her side

I may fall in love in a slippery slide.

◆
III

Somedays I think she is not for me

I fear to be vulnerable as a bee

as I worry to be her eyes's abductee

and I wonder if her treacherous smile could lead me somewhere,

a place where my heart feels free.

◆
IV

Yet that seems unlikely

as my saxophone blares a melody

that resonates my broken heart's fallacy

and my piano plays perfectly

in a key that only brings forth a melancholy.

◆
V

Whenever I see you merely sigh

I worry for you and for this heart's desire

then I feign a wry smile

as my heart wonders if it could ever reach you once in a while.

◆
VI

When I stroll on a rainy night

I reminisce an old love that seldom gives my heart a fright

I wonder if my silence could ever make a woman mine

I realised that my heartache was my only plight.

◆
VII

This rainy night, a soft rain seems to fall

bringing down sorrows that never dawn

and as a drip from leaves falls on my shoulder

I remembered a thorny rose in the rain,

tempting me to be engulfed into its own downfall,

a rose that was only for her, once upon a time, in an autumn's fall.

◆
VIII

Yet my heart craves for a love that it could for only once, pursue

someone new has bewitched me and flew

she is a woman that could fill this heart with something new

a woman that had hair and eyes like you

and my heart is ready to get away from you.

 

Yet there's a piece of you in how I dress

thus my heart is almost blue

and it could be only because of you

 

It's always been you.

A saxophone's confession.
