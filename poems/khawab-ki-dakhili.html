Khawab Ki Dakhili

When a dream walks in uninvited.

I

Kayi Raatein Guzar Gaye,

Uss bewaqt khwab ko dekhe

Hue, jisne mujhe mausamo

Mein badlav dikhana chhod diya.

◆
II

Wo Raat, ittar ki khushboo

Jaisi ladki mere khwaab

Mein ayi aur mere zaalim

Naseeb ki dastaan bangayi.

◆
III

Neele aur khule ambar

Ke neeche, aur baharon

Ki hawaon ki maujudigi

Mein, meine tumko paya.

◆
IV

Neele jharne ki tarah, tum

Bhi mere uss khwaab mein,

Neeli saari pehenke mere

Dil ki takdeere mitane ayi thi.

◆
V

Sadak pe tumhare saath

Chalte hue na waqt ka

Andaaza hua na lihaaz hua,

Bas tumhari hasi ka nasha hua.

◆
VI

Tumhare baalo ki lehero mein

Ghum hone ki saajish karte

Fir raha tha. Par tumhari aankho

Ne saajish ka mauka hi nahi diya.

◆
VII

Ye khwab tut gaya jab

Subah, baarish ki bundo

Ne mere khidki se, meri shayri

Ke panno mein dakhili paya.

◆
VIII

Ab kayi mausam beet gaye,

Ab kayi panchiyo se ishq

Karne ki koshish ki, par

Tum jaisi panchi nahi mili.

◆
IX

Na ab khwabo mein kahi

Dikhti ho na kisi kitabcha

Ke kone mein, aur na hi

Kisi akhbaar ke panno mein.

◆
X

Bas mere zindagi mein kahi

Bhi maujood ho, to meri nazm

Mein, aur mere shayrion mein

Jo ik kali ki intezaar mein hai.

◆
XI

Ab firse ye zindagi bahar

Leke aya hai, phool gulte

Hai to bas unmein tumhari

Hassi aur chhavi dikhti hai.

◆
XII

Ab mein iss patjhad ke mausam

Mein, inn pedo ke neeche, meri

Udaasi ko kalam mein, aur uski

Khayalo ko syaahi bana betha hun.

एक नज़्म — ख़्वाब की दाख़िली।
