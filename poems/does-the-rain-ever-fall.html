Does the Rain Ever Fall on Us

A conversation under the rain.

I

Does the rain ever fall on us as a sign?

She asked me in an apologetic tone

I wondered if it was raining in her heart or mine

while I stood beside her, blessed by her cologne.

◆
II

Does the windy rain make you shiver?

I asked her while the breezy wind blew

her hair that personified peace like a river

while I fell in love with eyes that were blue.

◆
III

Does the rain ever fail to embrace?

She asked me so nonchalantly

while I looked at her with a lonely face

and answered the question honestly.

◆
IV

Rain is my close friend indeed

it helps me hide my sorrowful tears

and helps me smile without any greed

that I had hid in my heart for years.

◆
V

I also often seek the early morning rain

and its haunting yet merciful pain

while I wondered if my heart could ever feign

for a grieving tomorrow, that follows me in the rain.

◆
VI

While the raindrops fell on her heavenly skin

I thanked God for this gracious day of July

while my fountain pen and I, live a life of sin,

as I wrote a small poem on a butterfly.

◆
VII

A silence engulfed in the air, and I dreaded

a haunting verse that came into my head

and as rain fell on this crooked shade

I wondered if I was actually living or dead.

◆
VIII

Did the rain take us on a journey yet?

I thought out loud as my love for her

spoke in silence and her hair that was wet

felt like a sight that could make me quiver.

◆
IX

When the rain untimely stopped

we stepped outside, from the shade

and the garden that was swamped

while I wished that we had stayed.

◆
X

Will we ever happen to meet on this long road?

I thought to myself as we bid adieu in charade

we went our separate ways, and as I started for my abode

I looked back and noticed a smile on her face.

◆
XI

A smile so hauntingly beautiful in that outwind

made my heart long for her and almost made me pursue

those feelings for her, that have now withered in the wind

leaving only memories, that I also meant to bid adieu.

◆
XII

Does the rainy sky ever fill you with hope?

I wondered as several seasons had went by

I prayed, that I could meet her once more on this life's slope

and to be in love with a girl that could make me sway.

◆
XIII

As I walk along this nostalgic garden

that had once helped me write a poem

now only flowers blossom here that seem golden

as the blue sky filled the scenery in tandem.

◆
XIV

I stroll under this blue and rainy sky

endless thoughts cross my poetic mind

I recall the memory of a girl from a July

while rain falls on me and my timid hind

 

I wished once more, under this shade

to meet her again in this rainy place

now I pray one more time, as I delayed

the thoughts that longingly chase,

 

for a simple twist of fate.

A conversation under the rain.
