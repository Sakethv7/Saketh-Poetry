Nathar Nazm

Jhuti Tasalli

The lies a poet tells himself.

Apne mann ki awaaz ko geet samajh, gaano mein uski yaadein dhundta fir, apne dil ki guzaarish ko suljhata firta hoon.

Apne aap ko shayar samajh, shayari suna suna kar, apne rooh ko jhuti tasalli de raha hoon.

Apne aap ko kavi samajh, uski aankhon ke saaya ko shabdo mein baya karne ki koshish hi karta reh gaya hun.

Apne aap ko aashiq samajh, kayi ankahi dastayein banane ki saazish kar betha hoon.

Iss sharab aur ye qalam se inn kagazon mein usko amar karne ka jurm apne bhooj pe lekhe bhatakte fir raha hoon.

एक नस्र नज़्म — झूठी तसल्ली।
