Ghaav Ki Khushboo

Even wounds carry a fragrance.

I

Jab tere yaad ke ghaav bharne lagte hai

Ek yaad ki khushboo fir mehsoos karne hoon.

◆
II

Baagh mein jab gulab ko titaliyan satati hai

Tab bahaane mein tumhe yaad karne lagta hoon.

◆
III

Kesa sannata chhaya hai iss ghar mein ya Rab

Har shaam sirf pal-e-tanhai ka hi tajurba kare ja raha hoon.

◆
IV

Kitabon ke beech jab jab apne ko khone laga

Tab tab zehen se fisli tazkaron ko inn panno mein pata hoon.

◆
V

Kyon sukoon ke pal ab sabab mangte hai

Aisi madhoshi hai ke nazm ab sukoon ke liye likhta hoon.

◆
VI

Ab kon hi fark kare sukh ya dukh mein

Madari reheke lamho mein bikhar gaya hoon.

◆
VII

Ab baagh mein phul to saare jhad gaye

Chalo, murjhaye phulon se pehechan phir kar leta hoon.

◆
VIII

Phool wapis khilne ka anjaam kabhi to denge iss baagh ko

Main to bas anjaam ke intezaar mein lamhe kata raha hoon.

एक नज़्म — घाव की खुशबू।
