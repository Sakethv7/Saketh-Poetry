Nazm

Naya Rang

A new colour fills the heart's shadow.

I

Aaj koi nayi kala dekhne ko mili hai

Kese kahe unki taraf aankhe bhatakne ko lagi hai.

◆
II

Unki justuju mein doob jaane ko mann hai

Ye bandagi koi nayi to nahi hai.

◆
III

Junoon mein aise khone ka shauq kabhi tha nahi

Fir aaj kyon unke husn ka junoon mujhpe sawaar hai.

◆
IV

Unki chandni baahein jo sama rang de

Ab mere dil ke saaye mein bhi rang bhar raha hai.

◆
V

Meri aankhe unko takte takte nahi thaki

Kher aankhe to sirf dil ka kehna maan rahi hai.

◆
VI

Unki ada mere pannon mein sama jaaye

Iss muddat ki ye hi khwaish hai.

एक नज़्म — नया रंग।
