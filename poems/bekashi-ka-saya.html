Bekashi Ka Saya

The shadow that homesickness casts.

I

Aaj kal sheher mein apne gaon ki yaad mein khoya sa rehta hoon

Lagta hai mere panno ko teri ada-e-bekashi phir chha gayi.

◆
II

Maine ek umr se apni kitaab mein sambhali hui hai

Wahi tasveer jo ek pal bhi nahi dekhi gayi.

◆
III

Gulshan murjha raha hai teri haseen ghataon ke bagair

So ab bhi mere dil mein teri yaadein kyo nahi murjha gayi.

◆
IV

Mere naseeb mein ek phool khilne ko laga sada ki mehrumi

Par aakhir mein na itminan hua na hi tujhe pyaar.

◆
V

Main jaan-e-bahaara tujhe bhulake khush hona chahta hoon

Par ye kambaqt yadaash dil ke liye khanjar bankar reh gayi.

◆
VI

Mujhe koi acha bhi nahi lagta humnaam tera

Ab koi tujsa nahi mila tha to pyaar se ummed chali gayi.

◆
VII

Par inn dino phir mausam beimaan sa lagne laga hai

Aisa afasana hai ke panno mein kisi naye ki ada-e-aafat si chha gayi.

एक नज़्म — बेकशी का साया।
