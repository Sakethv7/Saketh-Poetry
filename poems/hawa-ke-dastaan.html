Hawa Ke Dastaan

Stories the wind carries home.

Ae pichle mausam ke saathi

Ab ke baras aas paas bas tanhai hai.

Likhta hoon ghazal auron ke liye

Mere wo ghazal bhi tumhare naam hai.

Yun to apne nazuk se dil ke paas

Jaane kis kiske paighaam hai.

Koi puche to main kya bataun

Mere har khat tumhare naam hai.

Thak gaya hun mein iss bhag daud se

Par ruk gaya to yaad aata mera intezaar hai.

Pankhudi ki tarah bikhar jaati hai zindagi

Ye sakht ghadi bhi kitni rukhsaar hai.

Gaur se dekhun gulon ke kaanto ko to log haste hai

Zakhmon par gaur karna bhi ab haraam hai.

Jinn par apni saari khushiyon ko kiya nisaar

Aaj wo hi ehd-e-tanhai ke jimmewaar hai.

Usko fursat nahi milti ke wo palat ke dekhe

Hum hi bedilli mein doobke berozgaar hai.

Kitaab ke beech aaj bhi uski tasveer hai

Aankhein aaj bhi karti dard-e-karobaar hai.

Main kyun aisi tanhai se juda hoon.

Ye tanhai to ab meri dil-o-jaan hai.

Logon ke beech khudko paata hoon

Magar dil akela bitata gham-e-shaam hai.

Tumse pehele khat jo likhte the

Ab wo saare hawa ke dastaan hai.

Aur tumhare baad ke jo khat likhta hoon

Wo to tumhare khushbu ke nishaan hai.

एक ग़ज़ल — हवा के दास्तान।
