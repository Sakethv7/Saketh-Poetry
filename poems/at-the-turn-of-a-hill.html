At the Turn of a Hill

A chance encounter

I

The flower shop at the turn of a hill

Keeps all the pretty flowers at one place

While the cold wind in a shrill

Flips the pages of my book that is with me for grace

◆
II

Her hair flows like the sea

Displaying each curve of her wavy form

I wonder if it really is autumn by this tree

As her presence seems like a spring's affair

◆
III

The clouds that were darkening over me

Were banished by the winds of her stride

I was mesmerised by her eyes that fled

From mine that looked at her with delight

◆
IV

Flowers of jasmine danced at the tune of her emotions

I could notice she had an epiphany

As her hands were flipping a book's pages

And I couldn't help but smile at her symphony

◆
V

A hint of blush on her cheeks

As our eyes meet at the romance shelf

I pick a novel which reminisces a past love

Wondering if there is a future on this hill as I dwell

◆
VI

On the hope that shall rescue me from this shrill

Hinting me towards a new beginning

Guiding me through the end of this misery

I was reborn when I saw her at the turn of a hill

◆
VII

It was enough for me to concede

And saunter ahead with a blank slate

While I share this coffee with her that is sweet

Making me hopeful for a turn of fate.

Where flowers bloom and hope returns.
