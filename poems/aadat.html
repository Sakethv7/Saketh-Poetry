Aadat

What the heart grows used to.

I

Aaj kal unse milkar baat karke

Hume yu hi dil behelane ki aadat hai.

◆
II

Hawaon mein leherate hue bahaar ko

Humse guftugu karne ki aadat hai.

◆
III

Baarish ka mausam aaye to kher

Hume chhata bhoolne ki aadat hai.

◆
IV

Patjhad ke mausam ki tanhai ko

Humse shayari likhwane ki aadat hai.

◆
V

Afsaano mein unse door hokar

Hume purane khayalon ko anjaam dene ki aadat hai.

◆
VI

Ab majboori mein unhe bina mude

Humse door jaate dekhne ki bhi aadat hai.

एक नज़्म — आदत।
