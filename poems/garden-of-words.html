Garden of Words

A waka exchange, inspired by the film Garden of Words (2013).

Original — Kojiki

A distant rumble of thunder,

clouded skies.

Perhaps rain comes again —

if so, will you stay with me?

Her reply

A distant rumble of thunder.

Even if rain comes not,

I will stay here with you —

and if it rains, would you stay too?

The poet, to no one in particular

Even if it doesn't rain,

I'll be here,

waiting for you,

or someone like you.

"Even if it doesn't rain, I'll be here — waiting for you or someone like you."
