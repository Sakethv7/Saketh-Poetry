Only the Garden Roses Were Listening

A secret kept by the garden.

I

A day of autumn laden with flowers

is accompanied by a sorrowful night

hampered with scattering tears

in echoes of old memories in plight.

◆
II

A hopeful afternoon is spent in a bus

sightseeing and travelling around the city,

while emotions crash on a dubious

humming tune that remains blue and weary.

◆
III

I sauntered under the autumn sky

in search for an October's wish

that would reverse a day of July,

a day when she left in anguish.

◆
IV

I strolled empty streets in the evening

I looked upon a tree with orange leaves

that made me reminisce a wandering

woman whose beauty could rival a lily.

◆
V

Seasons ago we met without a choice,

my voice sounded like the rusting

leaves in front of her enigmatic voice,

that was calm even when it was raining.

◆
VI

She told me to not be so mysterious

as we dawdled through a garden

I wondered if she really knew

my love for her, hidden in my heart's den.

 

Only the garden roses were listening

she whispered near the garden of lily,

I wondered if fate was recurring

or hinting me of another misery.

◆
VII

Seasons felt like days with her

but now, without her, it's an eternal

autumn of sorrow in October

when even a new love is fatal.

◆
VIII

I boarded a nostalgic train back,

but these memories come and go

in this benign yet regretful month

where only bittersweet memories flow.

A secret kept by the garden.
