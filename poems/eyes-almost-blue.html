Eyes Almost Blue

She carried your eyes in a different face.

I

One rainy evening,

I heard an old tune from a street

I stood under a shade as heavy rain came down in a heartbeat

I lit a cigarette for my pain that almost made my heart weep.

◆
II

This rain brings memories wrapped in tears

I realised that the poems I write have always been about you,

a woman of my past, a memory that nobody shares.

◆
III

My eyes closed as I listened to that melody

a melody that had reminisces of your melancholy

memories that linger in this symphony

and cling onto a breeze that resembles my heart's fallacy.

◆
IV

A vendor smiles and sways to this melody's magic

a simple twist of fate had made its tactic

as I caught a glimpse of a woman who could prove that love is in itself, tragic

the rain was spinning a story that had no logic

I realised I may fall in love with her and felt pathetic.

◆
V

I caught a glimpse of her eyes that were almost blue

my heartbeat stopped for that moment when she looked into my eyes too

I became eager to write about her and this scenery as it all felt too good to be true.

◆
VI

My heart felt light yet heavy

I had forgotten this feeling that has retraced back to me as my heart felt a bit rainy

I see her drying her wet hair under the same shade, while she wore a smile that could wound any man that seemed dreamy

we both looked at the street that has a past memory.

◆
VII

This woman who stands five feet beside me carries a peculiar reminiscent of you

I know that to be absurd yet my heart feels it to be true

her blue eyes look my way and I fear it might only be you

my eyes look into hers that moment and just as a car passed by us, my eyes flew.

◆
VIII

For the first time, I didn't despise the rain

in the past it only showed my unbearable pain

fate always felt cruel, twisting my life as it only made tragedy remain

but now I stand beside a woman that makes me want to fall in love again.

◆
IX

I saw people write about love but I thought it was not meant for me

all I have managed to write about is heartbreak as my heart is yet to be free

now as I talk with this woman, I hope to make my heart a guarantee

a guarantee of a love that could remain within me and without me.

◆
X

We both smiled with glee

we looked at the rain drenched streets where people just flee

the rain was heavy and our umbrella was as useless as a bee

I started falling in love with her and with this place that had made me its abductee.

◆
XI

We waited for the rain to become calmer

yet I prayed for the rain to be harsher

 

And again we stood waiting

for a memory that was yet to be reaching

then the melody made me start humming

a tune that prompted me of you and your eyes that were always shining

I realised that I hadn't forgotten about you although I shouldn't be longing.

◆
XII

I stood there, calming my heart

I braced myself for another wound

as I had decided to find a love for this woman that has made my heart fall apart

 

She is reading an anthology while sharing glances with me in silence

my heart beats as fast as I can imagine and runs out of patience

as I look at the rain, I call out a poem from the book she was reading in solace.

 

"A faint clap of thunder clouded skies perhaps rain comes again if so, stay with me?"

 

She smiles in a calm manner and replies

 

"A faint clap of thunder even if rain comes not I will stay here together with you and if it rains, I wonder if you would stay here too?"

 

My mind went blank in joy, then I perceived a reply that I had given to you several seasons ago, to this same verse.

But I couldn't say it out loud as it seemed like a curse.

Yet I said it in my mind, as it wasn't something I could just subside.

"Even if it doesn't rain, I'll be here, waiting for you or someone like you."

 

I stood there, feeling guilty of falling for someone other than you

yet she feels almost you

I saw your eyes in her that are almost blue

yet I still wait for a simple twist of fate that could for once, make it all true.

She carried your eyes in a different face.
