Pehele Aur Fir

First one thing, then another

I

Unki hasi jo bahaaron ke phool sajade

Mujhe pehele aashiqi, fir maayusi, aur fir tanhai mein dhakel gaye.

◆
II

Chup chupke unhe dekhaa karte the,

Dekhte dekhte pehele dilkash, fir aashiq aur fir shayar ban gaye.

◆
III

Aap to nazdeek se bhi kitne jhut bolte rahe

Pehele ek baar, fir do baar, fir kayi baar dil tod gaye.

◆
IV

Din ba din badhti gayi, iss zindagi ke zulm hum pe

Pehele gulshan, fir gul-badan, fir gul murjhake jhad gaye.

◆
V

Haule haule unke saare taqaluff mit gaye

Aap se, phir tum hue, fir tum ke baad tum kaun ho gaye.

◆
VI

Aisa tha kya unke andaaz mein

Jo pehele, mann mein, fir soch mein aur fir panno mein sama gaye.

A nazm of transformation — how one person rewrites you, step by step.
