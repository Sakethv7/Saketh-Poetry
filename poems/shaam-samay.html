Shaam Samay

A glimpse of the moon

I

Shaam samay, door kisi suraj mukhi ke bageeche mein

Chand ko utarte dekha humne,

Chhand bhi kesa? Pura chand.

◆
II

Aankhon mein bhi, chitwan mein bhi, chand hi chand jhalakta hai

Chaand hi bindi, chaand hi jhoomar, chehara chaand aur hasi bhi chaand.

An evening's glimpse — the moon everywhere and in everything.
