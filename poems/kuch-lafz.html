Kuch Lafz

A ghazal of words that remain

I

Kuch lafz aaj bhi kisi ke naam reh gaye

Din ba din lafz kam safaat zyada beh gaye.

◆
II

Yun patjhad tanhai mein guzaarke

Hum sardi ka hausla badhaate gaye.

◆
III

Aaj gulshan se guzarte hue ek khayal aya

Kuch khayal jo raat ko sataake subha chale gaye.

◆
IV

Tere gham ko jaan ki talaash thi

Talaash mein hum gham-o-jaan ho gaye.

◆
V

Jab gham ke waje khatm hote rahe

Tab tumhari yaadein wapis aake theher se gaye.

◆
VI

Aisi bhi kya bekhudi hai Saketh

Itna bebas fir ek bahaar ke liye ho gaye.

Words addressed to someone — they remain long after the page is turned.
