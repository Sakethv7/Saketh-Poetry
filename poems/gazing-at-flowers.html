Gazing at Flowers

Life goes on within you and without you.

We were gazing at the flowers as we wryly smiled through the darkness in our hearts, searching desperately for the light that could guide us through our paths.

Birds fly away from the melancholy of my life as I hide my grief and the tears behind this mask I wear on my face.

The peaceful birds which had become accustomed to the silence inside of me, now are desperate to rejoice in the thrill which I have long lost.

The pain in my heart which was like a nonchalant voice, now becomes a solace for this old soul's void.

Everything which was of importance in my life, now feels mundane as I plunge out of the depths of my longing sorrows, yet I fall shortly after, into another depthless void of grief and bittersweet hollows.

I often feel as if we all are travellers, looking for a purpose or solitude amidst the inclemencies in our life's purposes. The love that we all possess, guide us through this vast ocean of emotions, acting like a passage, steering us through our agitations.

This solitude which we seek, is rather diminishing as we hide the emotions which make us look weak. With time, we come to terms with the magic we all have. All the love, peace and calm that we seek is already in us, we don't need to search for it as the great passage of words and emotions guide us to our own heart and we realise that, life goes on within you and without you.

Life goes on within you and without you.
