Jaayega Kahan Ab

A ghazal of the wanderer's question

I

Ye chand lamho ki baarish bhi ab

Kisi aur ke chhat pe baras gayi ab.

◆
II

Tumhare jaane anjaane ne kar diya bura haal

Tere mere sapne kabhi ek rang na honge ab.

◆
III

Jab dukh sukh baantne ke liye sahaara dhundhta fira

Par var diga tune bhi meri baat suni nahi ab.

◆
IV

Jiske liye sher-o-shayari se guftugu fir shuru ki thi

Wo hi mere kalam tutne ka wajah bana ab.

◆
V

Ye raat ye chandni fir kaha,

Saketh tu bhi ruton ka ghulam ban chuka hai ab.

◆
VI

Har cheez mein jo tu patjhad aur bahaar dhunde firta hai

Wahaan kon hai tera, musafir,

jayega kahan ab?

◆
VII

Waqt ko zaya kar kitne gham-e-shaam bitayega ab?

Teri manzil hai yahi, jaayega kahan ab?

A ghazal of the wanderer who searches for meaning in every season.
