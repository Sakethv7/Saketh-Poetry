Anjaan Sheher

Life in an unknown city.

I

Jaane kesi bandagi hai mujhe dastaan

Ek pal ki khushi ne umr bhar ke ghaav diye.

◆
II

Jis se bedilli karne ki koshish ki

Usne dua mein mujhe khoye hue armaan diye.

◆
III

Kya hi bataye haal puchne walon ko

Roz baagh mein gulab ki pankhudi tod diye.

◆
IV

Ab kaise gair ko apne jazbaat ada kare

Uss din ek baar batake gunnah to kar diye.

◆
V

Teri galiyon mein hoke hum badnaam

Kitne shehron mein apne nek naam kar diye.

◆
VI

Wo jo taameer hone wali thi bahaaron mein

Wo imarat ab barsaat mein khaali kar diye.

◆
VII

Ye dil ishq ke intezaar mein khala raha

So guftugu ki talaash mein dil fir khalvat kar diye.

◆
VIII

Zindagi iss anjaan sheher mein ab kese basar hogi

Hum to hurbat ke bagair hosh kho diye.

◆
IX

Nahi hai ab kisi aur ke liye soz-e-intezaar

Chhoti si zindagi ab tanhai mein guzaar diye.

एक नज़्म — अनजान शहर।
