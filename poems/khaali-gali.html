Khaali Gali

Empty lanes, empty evenings.

Aaj kal mere gali se to koi nahi guzar raha

To hum gali gali se guzar rahe hai kisi ke liye.

Gul phir khilne ko bechain hai iss bahaar-e-gulshan mein

Dil-e-nadaan jald baazi mein kuch nahi hai paane ke liye.

Apni khuli zulfe lapetke bethi hai ek husn pari samne

Kaise batlaye ke ab kuch nahi iss dil mein dene ke liye.

Uske sawalon ka jawaab tha to magar diya nahi

Hass aur diye ashk aankhon ke chupane ke liye.

Lagta hai ab uske ishq mein naye tajurbe dekhne ko milenge

Par aisa to nahi ke ye raste naye mere kalaam ke liye.

Apni befikri mein guzar jao meri jaan

Humara ek din ka sath jo thehra wo kaafi hai zindagi bhar ki yaadon ke liye.

Ek aur ajnabi haseena se guftugu ki arzoo to hai

Par beqarari ae dil tujhe na hua waisa sa kisi aur ke liye.

एक ग़ज़ल — ख़ाली गली।
