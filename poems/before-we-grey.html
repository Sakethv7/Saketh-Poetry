Before We Grey

Four years. Before our hair turns grey.

I

At a distance, autumn is green

But my heart keeps feeling blue.

Although life spent here was routine

Yet these memories always seem new.

◆
II

Now its the twilight days of school

The memories seem surreal

All the friends made are still cool

And these four years, we did only dream.

◆
III

Maybe we meet again in an autumn

When our hair may have turned grey,

And as we tell our stories with optimism

I hope these memories don't fade away.

These four years — I hope these memories don't fade away.
