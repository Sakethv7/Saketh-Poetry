Udaasi Ka Itiraf

A confession the heart makes only to itself.

Ye musafir jaam se pehele nazar fere nahi

Hum shaam ko piye bina kahi nikle nahi.

Yuhin mausam ki ada dekh ke yaad aya

Iss qismat ka kabhi tha saath nahi.

Gayab ho jaate hai ye nazmein aangan se har shaam

Ab to apne tanhai ka bhi sahaara nahi.

Agar muhabbat fir hoti qismat mein likhi

To iss bahaar ka hota itna afsos nahi.

Gulshan ko kitna bhi paani daalo

Inn gulon ke fitrat mein titliyan nahi.

Ab bedilli kaha chhodegi mera sahaara

Uske liye intezar karna tak to chhoda nahi.

Dil chidiyon ki aawaaz parakh leta hai

Itne aziyat jhelne ke baad dil ab nadaan nahi.

Log taquluff ko bhi samajte hai ikhlas

Har koi haath milaane wala dost nahi.

Jheel ke bahaav mein beh jaate hai har raat

Hum dil se nikale hue log ek jagah tikte nahi.

Raat se pehele hote hum jaam ke paas

Subha se pehele chhodte hum saath gilaas ka nahi.

Agar tum mil bhi jaate to ye afsaana khatm hota nahi

Zindagi mein to naye afsaano ki koi kami nahi.

Aisi udaasi ab kaha milti hai Saketh,

Ab to maan lo tumhe isse nafrat nahi.

एक ग़ज़ल — उदासी का इतिराफ़।
