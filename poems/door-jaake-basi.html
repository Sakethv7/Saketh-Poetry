Door Jaake Basi

A ghazal of longing across distance

I

Tu jab door jaake basi to mere qareeb lagi

Khuda hi jaane ye dil ab kya mod lega.

◆
II

Rubaru meri dhadkan bhi samajhne lagi

Ke tujhse milane ko sama ab kayi muddat lega.

◆
III

Ek hi jijhak raha haal-e-dil sudhaarne mein

Ke ye fasaana fir kahi tera zikr lega.

◆
IV

Jo apne dil pe guzari hai wo koi kya jaane

Isi umeed mein jeele ke dil tujhe bhula lega.

◆
V

Ab kese kisi se marasim badhaye hum

Yahi dar hai ke koi marasim tujh tak pahuncha lega.

◆
VI

Fir koi nayi pur-kashish qareeb aa rahi hai

Iski ada gulshan se jaane mein waqt lega.

◆
VII

Dil-e-nadaan iss halchal ka pata bata

Jo ishq dekar mera dard lega.

◆
VIII

Bahaar kata rahe hain chalte chalte

Afsos ye gulshan meri yaadein lega.

◆
IX

Sahulat se guzar jao meri jaan

Ye mohabbat sach mein meri jaan lega.

Distance that brings someone closer — and a heart that refuses to learn.
