Sardi Ka Mahina

A month that returns with its memories.

Sardi mein hi yaadein karti hai wapsi

Jo uss rahguzar mein kho gaye the shayad.

Aankhon ki mukhbiri ne kuch baatein kahi thi

To wo jaan hi gaya hoga hume unse pyar hai shayad.

Hazaar baar unse guftugu hui thi lekin

Hazaar baar kuch baat reh gayi thi shayad.

Lagta nahi ke patjhad ko humse koi gila hai

Par patton ki tarah hum to akele reh gaye shayad.

Naye gulon se jaan-pehechaan kaha hogi

Ae dil, unn zulfon ko tu bhula nahi shayad.

Zindagi bhar na bhulunga iss gham ko

Anjaam-e-aashiqi ka anjaam hi hai shayad.

Hum tauba karke mar gaye the be-maut

Tumhare aankhon ki maikashi thi shayad.

Jisse bichhda hai ab use bhul jaa Saketh

Uske khat uska intezar karte honge shayad.

Aaj fir usi rahguzar par khada hun

Jo kuch mausam pehele nakara tha shayad.

एक ग़ज़ल — सर्दी का महीना।
