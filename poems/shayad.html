Shayad

A ghazal of perhaps

I

Likhe the khat khwaabon mein bhi tumhe

Ek nazm tumhe aur dikhana hai shayad.

◆
II

Sher-o-shayari karte rahe sheher sheher

Ab bhi yaadein baaki reh gayi shayad.

◆
III

Tumhari kitaabe jhaankti hai almaari se

Unko na padhne mein hi bhalai hai shayad.

◆
IV

Ye qalam dhunta hai fir nayi si bedili ko

Mujhse ek naya paigam likhwayega shayad.

◆
V

Bebasi hai par ankahi chuban si hai,

Bade sheher mein ab ghutan si hai shayad.

◆
VI

Tumhari gali se guzre to aankhe bhar aayi

Tumhe bhulaane mein abhi aur waqt lagega shayad.

◆
VII

Khaas hum bhi the kisi ki nazron mein Saketh,

Magar nazar ki khwaishein badalne mein waqt nahi laga shayad.

◆
VIII

Gale lagna ho muhabbat ko to abhi lage

Der ho gayi to uske bagair jee lagne lagega shayad.

A ghazal built on uncertainty — perhaps the most honest word in Urdu.
