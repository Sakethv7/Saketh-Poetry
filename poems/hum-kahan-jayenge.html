Ghazal

Hum Kahan Jayenge

A ghazal of parting

I

Hai qayanaat-e-mehfil saji humare liye

Tum kaha jaoge, hum kaha jayenge.

◆
II

Logon ki to aadat hai bichhadna

Lapata sab hai par, hum kaha jayenge.

◆
III

Raatein kat ti nahi ab pehele jaisi

Tere khyaal mein doob kar, hum kaha jayenge.

◆
IV

Ab wo peheli si guftugu nahi tumhare saath

Unn palon ko yaad karke, hum kaha jayenge.

◆
V

Koi hasil na tha aarzu ka magar

Be-aarzu tumse alag hogaye, hum kaha jayenge.

◆
VI

Kabhi kabhi yun lagta hai ke tum laut aayega

Par ab mile bhi to hum kaha jaayenge.

◆
VII

Dil se dil milana chahta hoon firse lekin

Dil-o-jaan to tumne rakh liya, hum kaha jayenge.

◆
VIII

Ek hi baat hai jo ek baras se kehena chaaha

Dil ki baat kehekar bhi, hum kaha jayenge.

◆
IX

Tum kyon phirse inhi raston mein mujhe chhod aaye

Ab yahaan se wapis hum kaha jayenge.

◆
X

Ab bikharne ko hai ye teri meri daastaan-e-rang-o-bu

Tum kaha jaaoge hum kaha jayenge.

A ghazal of two roads that diverge — and neither knows the way.
