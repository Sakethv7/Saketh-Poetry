A Ring in the Rain

Love seen through a curtain of rain.

I

While I walk under these lush green trees

that were blessed with rain

they make me perceive an emotion

that resembles my pain.

◆
II

Sometimes I feel like crying

without any idea or reason

even the clouds shower with rain

on this world filled with treason.

◆
III

As I stroll beside these lonely streams

I realised how the world changed

while time flows as it seems

with clusters of emotions left stranded.

◆
IV

I sit on the banks of this deserted river

I notice how the slight drizzle of the rain

and the cold breeze makes me shiver

even the muffler she had given hadn't softened the pain.

◆
V

I look upon this rain

which drenches me in anguish,

I wonder if it could for once, fain

and I grin, for my nostalgic wish.

◆
VI

During the ending of one summer

I had fallen in love too bold

now comes a summer that reminds me of her

and of a love which once was new but now has grown old.

◆
VII

A heartsick smile appeared on my face

as I sauntered among the rain in autumn

I found a place to sit under this shade

a place that I had forgotten

◆
VIII

I walked up to it where I saw pieces of mozzarella

just to find her sitting there

with her hands gripping a blue umbrella

a serene silence engulfed the lair.

◆
IX

I saw the ring on her finger

an anguish filled my mind

I felt a grief that still seemed to linger

while the rain hid my tears in hindsight.

◆
X

Her hair had seemed to have grown longer

while she seemed so glad to see me

I just smiled and waited for a harbinger

who could be the one to hear my plea.

◆
XI

I recalled the scars that she once left plentiful

she still is the woman whom I can't forget

I saw her again and it feels like a curse too beautiful

and she comes anchored with a love in regret.

◆
XII

I bleed through my fingers

that were once gruesomely rough

now I walk away from a love that shivers

a love that has tortured me long enough.

Love seen through a curtain of rain.
